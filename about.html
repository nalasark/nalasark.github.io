About Me

programmer, designer, storyteller.

If you can dream it, you can do it. ~ Disney

Hi, I'm Alan! Welcome to my ark of works and ideas. I am a recent graduate from SUTD, majoring in Computer Science, with an invested passion in design, immersive media and filmmaking.

I am an immersive creative technologist, working with companies like MeshMinds to create engaging immersive experiences, and co-leading SUTD Immersive Realities Lab to pioneer advancements in immersive technology for education & research.

One of my greatest joys is in being able to share ideas and make them happen; to tell stories in whatever work I do.

Demonstrating my research in gesture to the board of directors at URA.

I've always taken the road less travelled. From pursuing computer science in high school, starting a social entrepreneurship, to researching immersive media in university. Fearlessly pursuing what is not normally taught, has often made every difference.

Presenting Sleep Taut at HDB Cool Ideas (Uni) Competition, clinching 1st place.

I believe a great product comes from a team that is inclusive and expressive. In a team, I bring creative engagement, reliability, and a steadfast ability to grow.

Volunteer Program to Laos, with the class that my team taught.

Empathy is a core value in all my designs. I hope through my works, or the stories I tell, I can make the world a better place, a step at a time.
