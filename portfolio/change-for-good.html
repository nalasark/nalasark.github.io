Change For Good

Co-Founder, Creative Lead

Social experiment to prove that Singaporeans are kinder than we think.

Simly pay forward your small change after buying a drink at a vending machine & let it accumulate into a big change, into a free drink for a lucky someone.

A team of fresh graduates out of high school, we had only our common drive to make a difference in this 4 months til university.

Initially bounced around & braving nights making our prototype towards uncertainty, we pitched & successfully garnered $2000 seed funding & several sponsorships.

As the creative lead, I handled graphic designs for our product and publicity, our website and produced the commercial film.

In the end, it is the smile on the people's faces that made it most worthwhile :)
