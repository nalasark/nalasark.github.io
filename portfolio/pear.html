Project PEAR

SUTD Game Lab

Note: Project PEAR is currently still in development and the video showcase above is not the final version of the game.

Project PEAR utilizes geolocation and AR to create an immersive, educative game on climate change action.

Players, together with their PEAR, go around tackling climate change problems, through mini games in AR and building solutions on the map.

AR is a very powerful tool to inculcate behavioral changes as actions taken in AR mirror closely to actions taken in real life. Through addictive minigames that each addresses an issue, we aim to positively alter players' awareness and behavior towards these issues.

As the lead programmer, I developed the AR pet interaction features, and the AR minigames that are still in development.
