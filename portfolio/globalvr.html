After winning the Singapore VR Hackathon, we arrived in Shanghai, ready to take on the global stage. There are 12 teams from across the globe, ranging from USA, New Zealand, to China. Each team were winners and finalists of their own local hackathon.

For the competition, the theme was "Numbers/Digits". Each team was randomly assigned a technology to implement - hand tracker, Vive tracker, and eye tracker.
We got the uSens Fingo hand tracker.

Brainstorming and rapid prototyping the experience.
