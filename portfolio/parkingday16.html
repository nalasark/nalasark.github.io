Parking Day 2016

URA x SUTD, Project Director

Annual community design exhibition where parking lots are transformed into liveable spaces.

Parking Day Singapore began from humble roots. In 2013, SUTD brought the event to 1 street in Singapore. In 2014, URA took the idea and made it an annual event. Finally in 2016, SUTD came on board to become official organisers with URA.

As the Project Director from SUTD, I headed a team of 25, organising exhibition at 3 central districts and initiating the school outreach & mentorship programme, with 4 local art colleges and over 200 exhibitors.
